Gran's Bread Rolls

★★★★★

Servings: 18 rolls Source: docs.google.com

Ingredients

567g (5 3/4 cups, 1 1/2 lb, 567g) white bread flour

1 Level teaspoon salt

64 g (2 1/4 oz) marg

1 sachet yeast

1 1/4 cups (1/2 pint) milk

2/3 cup (1/4 pint) boiling water

Directions

Put the flour and salt in a bowl

Rub the marg into the flour

Mix the yeast in

Mix in the water and milk

When it has turned into dough put on a board and knead for 5 mins until stretchy

Put in floured bowl cover with warm damp cloth and leave to rise until it doubles in size.

Put on the board and divide into 18 rolls

Put into a greased and floured baking tray leaving room to spread covering with a warm damp cloth

Leave until double in size

Put at 220°C for about 12 minutes. Tap its bottom’ if it is firm and sounds hollow’ then it is done (the sides may still be soft).
