Frozen Roasted Vegetables

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Yield: 2 - 3 servings Source: buildyourbite.com

Ingredients

12 oz mixed frozen vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, carrots)

2 tablespoons olive oil

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon pepper

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper

Pour frozen vegetables onto parchment paper

Toss vegetables with olive oil and spices, until coated

Bake frozen vegetables at 400 degrees for around 30 minutes, until edges begin to brown (stir/flip every 10 ish minutes)
